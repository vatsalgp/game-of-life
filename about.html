Game of Life

About

  • The game is a zero-player game, that is its evolution is determined by its initial state, requiring no further input.
  • One interacts with the Game of Life by creating an initial configuration and observing how it evolves.
  • It is Turing complete and can simulate any other Turing machine.
  • It was devised by John Conway who died on 11 April 2020 at the age of 82 due to COVID-19.
  • Wikipedia Link

Rules

  • Any live cell with two or three live neighbors survives.
  • Any dead cell with three live neighbors becomes live.
  • All other cells die or remain dead.

Mechanics

  • Click on a cell to toggle its state.
  • Press Enter for passage of time.
